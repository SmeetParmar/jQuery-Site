BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used tool to assess an individual's body weight relative to their height. It provides a quick indication of whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. BMI is not a direct measure of body fat, but it is an essential tool for health professionals to identify potential weight-related health risks.Body Mass Index (BMI) is a number calculated from a person's weight and height.

It is used as a simple and quick indicator of body fatness and is commonly used to classify individuals into different weight categories. Please note that BMI is a general indicator and does not take into account factors such as muscle mass, bone density, or other health conditions. It is always best to consult with a healthcare professional for a more comprehensive evaluation of your health and weight status.
